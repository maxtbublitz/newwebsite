About

Overstepping the boundaries of conventional design

Max Bublitz is a visual designer and programmer who specializes in graphic design, web design and development, and UI/UX design. Max graduated the University of Colorado, Boulder in 2021 with a Bacchelors of Science in Creative Technology Design (CTD). The CTD program blends arts and engineering, giving students a background in visual design and computer programming.


For his Senior Thesis, Max designed a semi-virtual interactive experience for children undergoing anethesia to lower their anxiety, and was able to sucessfully impliment the project during two elective surgeries. Max was most recently employed as a graphic and web designer and Vita Inclinata Technologies.
