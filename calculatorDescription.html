Savings Calculator

This calculator gives potential customers an estimated return on their investment based on five variables.

A CSV file is read to group items into makes and models. Names, values and branding have been randomized for client confidentialty.

UI Design

Figma was used to design the page. Two breakpoints were created to allow for desktop, tablet and mobile displays.
