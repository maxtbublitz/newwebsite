Simplified Product 3D Model

The model that engineering worked with was extremely complex, as it included every component in the product, down to the last screw. The grasshopper plugin in Rhinoceros 360 was used to model a simplified Vita Rescue System.

Composited Imagery

Because the product was new, not many photos of the product in action existed. Images were edited and composited to create more appealing and engaging imagery.
